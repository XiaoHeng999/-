# Loop Engineering Resources

## Knowledge

- [Article: "Loop Engineering Explained" — Louis-François Bouchard (What's AI)](https://www.louisbouchard.ai/loop-engineering/)
  最清晰的单篇入门解释。涵盖概念起源、Loop vs Automation、实际案例、成本问题和护栏。优先推荐阅读。
- [Guide: "Agentic Loops: From ReAct to Loop Engineering (2026 Guide)" — Data Science Dojo](https://datasciencedojo.com/blog/agentic-loops-explained-from-react-to-loop-engineering-2026-guide/)
  最全面的分类指南。涵盖 10 种 Loop 类型（ReAct、Reflexion、Ralph Loop、/goal 等）的演进历史和技术细节。适合深入了解 Loop 的分类学。
- [Article: "Design AI Loops That Ship While You Sleep" — Linas's Newsletter](https://linas.substack.com/p/loop-engineering-complete-guide)
  实践导向的完整指南，重点在如何设计能"在你睡觉时交付"的 Loop。
- [Article: "Loop Engineering at Enterprise Grade" — Truefoundry](https://www.truefoundry.com/blog/loop-engineering-enterprise-agent-runtime)
  企业级视角：审批、预算、追踪、可观测性。适合理解生产环境的考虑。
- [Article: "Humans and Agents in Software Engineering Loops" — Martin Fowler](https://martinfowler.com/articles/exploring-gen-ai/humans-and-agents.html)
  Thoughtworks 系列（2026年3月），探讨人与 Agent 在软件工程循环中的交互模式。
- [Blog: "Software Engineering in 2026" — Ben Congdon](https://benjamincongdon.me/blog/2025/12/29/Software-Engineering-in-2026/)
  2026 年软件工程趋势概览，包含 self-service 基础设施和 MCP 的讨论。
